VR chat

VR Chat has become a very popular way of communicating with many different communites; especially during these tough times. But when chatting online you must be careful of who you may be talking to. Make sure they have a valid username and listen for signs of a voice changer software.

sharing media

When uploading content to the internet you must be aware that nothing is ever erased from the internet. Even if you remove a post or a comment the web service you are uploading to more than likely has a database where its all saved.

online media

When going online and looking for a new service or website, you must be aware of some things. Not every website is secured! look for a little lock in the search bar, most modern browsers have a way to inform you to take caution.

chatting online

Just like posting content on the internet, chats online can always be saved by the web service you are on. If you are going to be chatting with an online friend try to make sure they are who they say they are. It is also very importnat to limit the amount of personal information you share.

gaming online

Gaming and enterntainment is an important distraction to have especially today. But online games and services are just like any other web service. Gaming is often a combination of chatting and sharing information with strangers so beware and try to enjoy your time online without revealing too much to your game mates.
